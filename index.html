About Me

Juan David Carvajal Agudelo
I am a biologist from Manizales, Caldas, Colombia, and I work in the field of molecular biology techniques to research and discover facts about biodiversity, and neotropic pathogens and diseases. I am also very interested and working in molecular biology research and bioinformatics, mainly to the description and analysis of microbiomes related with diseases and pathogen interactions.
You can find me as Juan D. Carvajal-Agudelo in research purposes.

From February 2022 to November 2025, I worked as a Research Assistant in the Bone Development Lab at Mount Saint Vincent University, Halifax, Canada, under the supervision of Dr. Tamara Franz-Odendaal, studying the molecular and cellular biology of bone development and the effects of simulated microgravity and vibration on zebrafish and chicken models.

I am currently recognized as Investigador Junior by the Ministerio de Ciencia, Tecnología e Innovación de Colombia (Minciencias), and I served as a scientific evaluator for Convocatoria 975 de 2026, reviewing master's-level research proposals.
